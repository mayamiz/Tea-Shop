We are an online-only tea shop located in the United States.

I'm Maya, the founder of Maya's Tea Shop. I started this store to share my love of tea with the world.

I noticed the lack of places to buy quality loose-leaf tea in the US, so I decided to start my own business.

We sell fair-trade teas and teaware from numerous different sources.
